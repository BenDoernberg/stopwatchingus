Not in DC? Here's how you can take part.

1. Sign up to be notified of viewing parties

Members of our coalition will be hosting viewing parties around the country. Sign up to be notified of events near you.

2. Donate (and get perks!)

We're running a campaign on Indiegogo that'll help fund the costs involved in running an event like this: things like hiring a sound system and stage, getting permits, and most importantly, getting buses from across the east coast to DC.

Perks include:

  • T-shirts, hoodies, and stickers!
  • Copies of the Pentagon Papers signed by Daniel Ellsberg.
  • Autographed copies of reddit-cofounder Alexis Ohanian's book "Without Their Permission."

3. Volunteer

We need your help!

Get involved by calling friends and family on the east coast and telling them to attend or by hosting a viewing party in your area.
